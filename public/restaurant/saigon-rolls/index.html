The Spotlight

Tucked along the iconic King Street strip in Old Town Alexandria, Saigon Rolls is bringing the bold, aromatic flavors of Vietnamese cuisine to one of Northern Virginia's most charming neighborhoods. Opened in early 2025, the restaurant is backed by the family behind the beloved Nha Trang Restaurant at Eden Center, so the culinary roots run deep. The menu centers on Vietnamese staples done right: rich, slow-simmered phở broths, crispy spring rolls, fresh summer rolls packed with herbs and rice noodles, and bánh mì sandwiches loaded with pickled vegetables and your choice of protein. Halal-conscious diners should ask about halal protein options, with the chicken-based dishes being the go-to picks here. The space itself is warm and cozy with wooden interiors, making it equally good for a quick weekday lunch or a sit-down dinner. Service is consistently praised for being friendly and attentive, and the generous portions make it easy to leave satisfied. Halal Rated Verdict: A solid spot for Vietnamese cravings in NOVA. Stick to the chicken phở, grilled chicken bánh mì, and vegetarian rolls for a halal-friendly experience. A hidden gem for the community.
