Halal Food in Sterling & Ashburn, VA

From late-night halal street food to Korean-Mexican fusion and halal pizza — the complete guide to halal food in Sterling and Ashburn, Virginia.

Sterling and Ashburn sit in Loudoun County — and together they form one of the most underrated halal food corridors in all of NOVA. The combination of late-night options, creative concepts, and legacy Desi spots makes this area worth the drive.

The Standouts

🌙
Naz's Halal Food
NYC-Style Street Food · Sterling, VA · ✓ Halal
NYC halal cart food in Sterling — open until 4AM. This is the late-night halal fix NOVA has been waiting for. Chicken over rice, lamb gyro, white sauce. If you know, you know.
🫕
Karahi Boys
Pakistani · Sterling, VA · ✓ Halal
If you're serious about karahi, Karahi Boys in Sterling is your answer. Proper Pakistani cooking — the karahi is rich, aromatic, and worth every bite. A community favorite for years.
🥙
SK Shawarma
Lebanese · Sterling/Ashburn, VA · ✓ Halal
SK Shawarma has earned a loyal following across Sterling and Ashburn locations. The chicken shawarma wrap is perfectly seasoned and packed tight — never disappoints.
🍕
Pizza Twist Ashburn
Indian-Inspired Halal Pizza · Ashburn, VA · ✓ Halal
Halal Indian-inspired pizza in Ashburn. Tikka masala base, paneer toppings, all halal. Surprisingly addictive and genuinely creative — one of NOVA's most original halal concepts.
🏔️
Lakshmi Chowk
Pakistani Street Food · Ashburn, VA · ✓ Halal
Pakistani street food culture brought to Ashburn. Lakshmi Chowk nails the chaotic, delicious energy of a real chowk — chaat, karahi, and everything in between.

Why Sterling & Ashburn for Halal Food?

Loudoun County has seen significant growth in its South Asian and Muslim community over the past decade. The result is an increasingly impressive halal restaurant ecosystem — with more independent concepts, later hours, and better quality than you'd expect from a suburban Virginia corridor.

🌙 Naz's Halal Food in Sterling is one of the only halal spots in NOVA open until 4AM — making it the undisputed late-night halal champion of Northern Virginia.
